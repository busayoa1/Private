# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 4, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our social media monitoring services.

## Information We Collect

### Social Media Data
We access the following information from your connected Instagram and Facebook accounts:
- Follower counts and growth metrics
- Engagement data (likes, comments, shares, saves)
- Post performance statistics
- Story and Reel analytics
- Reach and impressions data

### Account Information
- Social media account handles
- Page and account IDs
- Access tokens (stored securely and encrypted)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:
- Generating analytics reports
- Monitoring social media performance
- Providing daily performance summaries
- Identifying trends and growth patterns

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely with encryption
- Access tokens are never shared or exposed
- We do not store social media content (posts, images, videos)
- Only metrics and statistics are retained

## Data Sharing

We do not:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your information with advertisers
- Use your data for purposes other than monitoring

## Third-Party Services

We integrate with:
- Facebook/Instagram APIs
- GoHighLevel (if configured)

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Disconnect your accounts at any time
- Request deletion of your data
- View what data we have collected
- Revoke API access permissions

## Data Retention

- Analytics data is retained for 12 months
- Access tokens are refreshed periodically
- You can request data deletion at any time

## Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted with an updated date.
